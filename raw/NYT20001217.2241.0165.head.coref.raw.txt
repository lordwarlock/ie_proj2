<s> Booking Your Ticket :  </s>
<s>  Fly nonstop .  </s>
<s> The fewer connections you make , the lower the chance that you will suffer a delay .  </s>
<s>  Pick airports with care .  </s>
<s> If you have to connect , choose the least-congested airport possible ; some airports , like Chicago O'Hare and La Guardia in New York , experience chronic delays .  </s>
<s> Take into consideration the weather and the season ; an airport in the South might have fewer winter snowstorms but more spring and summer thunderstorms .  </s>
<s>  Check times between connecting flights .  </s>
<s> Airlines often book tight connections and do not take responsibility if you miss yours .  </s>
<s> Assume each leg of your flight will arrive late , and book connections that allow ample time .  </s>
<s>  Check on-time records .  </s>
<s> The Transportation Department requires major domestic airlines to make the on-time record for every flight available to consumers .  </s>
<s> You can check online at www.dot.gov .  </s>
<s> That site also reports each airline 's on-time record for the previous month .  </s>
<s>  Travel on off-peak days and at off-peak times .  </s>
<s> Saturday is usually the least-traveled day of the week ; so are major holidays .  </s>
<s> Monday mornings and Friday afternoons are peak travel times for business travelers .  </s>
<s> Flights leaving earlier in the day are less likely to be delayed , because of the cascading effect that disruptions have on airline schedules .  </s>
<s>  Use smaller airports .  </s>
<s> Instead of Los Angeles International , for example , consider flying into Burbank or John Wayne Airport in Orange County , Calif ., or use Westchester County Airport instead of JFK in New York .  </s>
<s>  Avoid electronic tickets .  </s>
<s> Though they are convenient and more secure than an easily lost paper ticket , they are not transferable between most airlines if you need to switch carriers because of delays .  </s>
<s>  Watch for labor disruptions .  </s>
<s> Check newspapers and Web sites like www.thenewfoundlandgroup.com to identify airlines experiencing labor strife , which often increases cancellations and delays .  </s>
<s>  Leave a number .  </s>
<s> Make sure the airline you book with has a phone number at which you can be notified of delays or cancellations .  </s>
<s>  Use a travel agent .  </s>
<s> A good one can be a great ally in the event of a problem  and may save you time and money .  </s>
<s> Day of Departure :  </s>
<s>  Be informed .  </s>
<s> Check your flight 's status before heading to the airport .  </s>
<s> Automated telephone services or airline Web sites often provide more up-to-date information than do ticket agents .  </s>
<s> The Federal Aviation Administration 's Web site , www.fly.faa.gov , has real-time information on operations at the biggest airports .  </s>
<s>  Pack defensively .  </s>
<s> Take along a change of clothes and a toothbrush in your carry-on bag .  </s>
<s> If traveling with an infant , take 24 hours ' worth of diapers and extra formula .  </s>
<s> When possible , avoid checking luggage , in case you need to switch flights or carriers .  </s>
<s> Ship your gifts ahead .  </s>
<s>  Educate yourself .  </s>
<s> Find out what your airline promises to do for passengers in the event of a delay .  </s>
<s> Print out a copy of the policy from the airline 's Web site , and carry it with you  in case the airline representatives need a reminder .  </s>
<s> If a Flight Is Delayed :  </s>
<s>  Keep your cool .  </s>
<s> Becoming angry at airline personnel is counterproductive .  </s>
<s> Sympathy for their plight will sometimes produce results .  </s>
<s>  Don't stand in line .  </s>
<s> To get on another flight , it is often more effective to call the airline from the airport than to wait at the ticket counter .  </s>
<s> Carry a cell phone ; if delays are widespread , public telephones may be in demand .  </s>
<s>  Check the competition .  </s>
<s> If another airline has seats to your destination , ask your airline to endorse your ticket to the other line .  </s>
<s>  Get what you paid for .  </s>
<s> If you paid full fare or are a high-mileage frequent flier , let the airline know .  </s>
<s> Your odds of getting a seat on the next flight out will increase , or the airline might pay for your hotel , if you are delayed overnight .  </s>
<s>  Consider driving .  </s>
<s> If the last leg of your flight is a 200-mile hop , renting a car might be a better choice than waiting out a delay .  </s>
<s> Getting Even :  </s>
<s>  Write a letter .  </s>
<s> The airlines have pledged to respond to consumer complaints within 60 days .  </s>
<s> They almost always offer a refund , reimbursement of expenses , a discount on your next ticket  or all three .  </s>
<s> Be sure to save the relevant paperwork .  </s>
<s>  Make it a federal case .  </s>
<s> Send a copy of your letter to Washington .  </s>
<s> Complaints are charged against each airline in the Transportation Department 's monthly Air Travel Consumer Report and serve as a basis for rule making and enforcement action .  </s>
